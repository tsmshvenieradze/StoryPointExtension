<objective>
Remove the `push: branches: [master]` trigger from `.github/workflows/ci.yml` so that ci.yml fires on pull-requests only. This is half of the "two-workflow split" — publish.yml (Plan 06-02) will own the master-push surface; ci.yml owns the PR surface.

Purpose: Defense-in-depth — a PR run physically cannot reach the (future P7) publish step because publish.yml has no `pull_request:` trigger and ci.yml has no `push:` trigger after this change. Also eliminates the doubled-CI-minutes pitfall (A4 in 06-RESEARCH.md) where master pushes would otherwise fire BOTH workflows.

Output: 1 modified file, 3 lines removed, no other changes.
</objective>
